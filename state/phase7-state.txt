  ReadWritePaths scoped, PrivateTmp, ProtectKernelTunables/Modules/ControlGroups,
  RestrictAddressFamilies (AF_INET, AF_INET6, AF_UNIX), LockPersonality,
  RestrictSUIDSGID.
